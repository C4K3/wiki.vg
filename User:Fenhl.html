Fenhl is a member of the Wurstmineberg server, and totally not a spambot. He likes to talk about himself in the third person.

Fenhl also runs the notifico bot that posts mainspace edit messages from this wiki to #mcdevs.

Projects

Fenhl started the Hematite server project. Hematite is a reimplementation of Minecraft in Rust, which can currently barely spawn a player. There's also a client, but Fenhl hasn't really contributed so far.

Other Minecraft-related projects Fenhl has contributed to include:

  • the Wurstmineberg Minecraft API
  • a fork of Mapcrafter's player markers plugin for non-Bukkit servers
  • The Protocol article, as well as Pre-release protocol
  • The Minecraft Wiki, most notably by pushing toward a more consistent article structure
